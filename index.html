Key Features

Find items

Find exactly the item that you need by looking through the available items in your area using a strong search feature and filters, that allow you to specify what you want and under which conditions. Select the item and immediately get into contact with the person lending it out.

List items

Kitley allows you to very easily put the items online that you want to let the people in your neighbourhood borrow from you, so they can have quick access to it when they need it. Perfect if you want to help out and strengthen your community!

Ping

Are you in a hurry and the item you need is not listed? Use a ping to notify people in your area and ask if any of them can help you out. Receiving these pings is also beneficial to you, because if you respond to a ping, you get in-app currency, which you can use towards buying pings yourself.

Inventory

Kitley offers a clear overview of what item you are currently lending out, to whom you are lending it to, and when you expect it to be returned. Similarly, Kitley also keeps track of what you are borrowing. This removes a lot of stress and uncertainty from the whole process.
