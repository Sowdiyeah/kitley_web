MARKETING PLAN

INTRODUCTION

Marketing campaign checklist:
  • Setting the Mission/Vision of the app
  • Setting the target group of customers
  • Find the right channels of reaching them
  • Start social media presence (instagram, facebook, etc)
  • Start a website which will be the main online presence
  • Start promoting the app phisically, by attending different events
  • Promote brand-related things (suggestive images, merch, etc)
  • Presentations in specific places (universities, offices, etc. so people can become more aware of the app)
  • Partner with complementary companies and do mutual marketing
